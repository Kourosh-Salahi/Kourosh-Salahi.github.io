Neural Radiance Fields

By Kourosh Salahi · CS180/280A: Computer Vision & Computational Photography

In this project, I build a complete NeRF pipeline from scratch: camera calibration, pose estimation, ray sampling, neural field construction, volume rendering, and training NeRFs on both the Lego dataset and my own captured object.

Part 0 — Camera Calibration & Dataset Capture

I used OpenCV’s ArUco system to detect markers, calibrate intrinsics, estimate extrinsics, and visualize camera poses in 3D using Viser.

Camera Frustums Visualization

These visualizations confirm correct pose estimation, correct orientation, and consistent intrinsics.

Part 1 — Neural Field Fitting (2D)

Model Architecture


MLP:
- Positional Encoding: L = 10 (42-dim input)
- Layers: 4 hidden layers
- Width: 256
- Activation: ReLU, final Sigmoid
- Optimizer: Adam (lr = 1e-2)
- Batch size: 10,000
